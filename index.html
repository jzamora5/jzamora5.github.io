About Me



I am a Full-stack developer fluent in Python, and JavaScript. I know how to use relational and non relational databases such as SQL, MongoDB and DynamoDB. I have developed web backends using Express, Flask, and Django, and frontends using ReactJS, Bootstrap, and JQuery. The latest project I have been working on has been a dynamic front end for my current company with ReactJS. For this project I have developed the user authentication views, and the user profile dashboard view.



On a more personal level I am someone in love with the audiovisuals field and the technological field. I enjoy spending my free time listening to music, learning new things, playing video games, and watching animated series. I am also a Sound Engineer and I have professional experience setting up equipment for different audiovisual events and also experience mentoring people in learning programming.



I am motivated by the idea of improving myself on a daily basis and becoming really skilled at programming. I feel that coding is really interesting and you develop important logical abilities that make you smarter. I also enjoy meeting interesting people you can learn from, and grow as a person by interacting with them. I believe this is important because in most cases you will have to work in teams for software development, and trying to make the most out of the experience is a must. I think I am pretty good at working with others and take a leadership role if necessary. I always hope to be someone reliable that people can count on and trust.
